    The research focus of our group is the process development for biofuel production using microbes as a cell factory. The areas which have been pursued currently are: biofuel production from microalgae; development of Clostridium sp. as a cell factory for butanol production; and engineering Zymomonas mobilis for the efficient production of biofuels from lignocellulosic biomass. In our lab we aim at improving overall performance of the technology via combined modifications at the process (Biochemical engineering approach) and strain level (genetic engineering approach). In our group we have expertise to create solutions for process development by combining biochemical and biological knowledge with engineering principles. For detail please refer to the research section.
